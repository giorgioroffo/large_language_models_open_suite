### Giorgio Roffo - Curriculum Vitae

#### Professional Summary
Giorgio Roffo is a Ph.D. holder and a Senior Research Scientist specializing in Artificial Intelligence, Deep Learning, and Computer Vision. He currently serves as an Applied Scientist at Cosmo Intelligent Medical Devices in Milan, Italy, where he focuses on developing AI-driven solutions for various applications in deep learning, artificial intelligence, and machine learning.

#### Professional Experience
- **2022 - Present: Senior Research Scientist, COSMO IMD, Milan**
  - Leading AI research in computer vision for medical devices, focusing on automated medical imaging analysis.
  
- **2020 - 2022: Research Scientist, Mind Vision Labs LTD, London**
  - Collaborated with UCL and Toyota Research Institute on advanced autonomous driving technologies under the supervision of Prof. Nilli Lavie, head of UCL Attention and Cognitive Control laboratory.
  
- **2017 - 2020: Research Associate, University of Glasgow**
  - Worked with Prof. Alessandro Vinciarelli on deep learning and attention models for social signal processing.
  
- **2014 - 2017: Ph.D. in Computer Science, University of Verona**
  - Thesis on "Ranking to Learn and Learning to Rank" under the supervision of Prof. Marco Cristani.
  
- **2012 - 2013: Research Student, Italian Institute of Technology (IIT)**
  - Supervised by Prof. Vittorio Murino and Dr. Loris Bazzani.
